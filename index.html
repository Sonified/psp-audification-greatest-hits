PSP Encounters Venus

Hear the sound of PSP passing through the Venusian ionosphere during its 4th flyby of Venus. The Venusian ionosphere is a glowing, electric layer of gas high above Venus where sunlight zaps the air and turns it into charged particles, kind of like the Sun is giving Venus a sparkly space crown! An audible "bloop" marks the entry, followed by intense outbound mystery waves.
